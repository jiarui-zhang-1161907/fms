Welcome to the Te Waihora Farm Management Simulator (FMS)!

Te Waihora FMS is a system designed to model stock management on a farm. The primary goal is to help farmers simulate the optimal number of livestock (such as cows) that their farm can sustain based on the available pasture (grass).

This tool provides valuable insights into:

  • The current number of animals on the farm.
  • The average weight of the livestock.
  • How much pasture is available for consumption.
  • Strategies for managing stock movements and feeding schedules.

By using this simulator, farmers can make informed decisions regarding:

  • Stock density and distribution across different paddocks.
  • Optimal times for moving stock to new pastures.
  • Supplemental feeding requirements during periods of low pasture growth.

Our system aims to balance the needs of the livestock with the sustainable use of pasture resources, ensuring both the health of the animals and the productivity of the farm.
